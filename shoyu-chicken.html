Homemade Corn Dogs

Review: I love the corn dogs you buy at carnivals but wanted to make them at home so I created this easy batter for frankfurters. Great served with mustard.

Ingredient

  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger root/li>
  • 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika (Optional)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 5 pounds skinless chicken thighs

Directions

    Gather all ingredients.

    Combine cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl; stir in milk and egg to make a batter.

  • Step 3

    Heat oil in a deep fryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  • Step 4

    Meanwhile, pat frankfurters dry and insert a skewer into each one.

  • Step 5

    Roll frankfurters in batter until well coated.

  • Step 6

    Fry 2 or 3 corn dogs at a time in preheated oil until lightly browned, about 3 minutes.

  • Step 7

    Drain on paper towels.

  • Step 8

    Serve and enjoy!
